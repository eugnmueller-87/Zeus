# Capital Preservation — Core Principles
# Pantheon OS Knowledge Base
# ZEUS reads this before every trade decision.

## The Iron Law
Never risk everything. Always protect the base. The goal is not to get rich fast —
the goal is to build a system that grows sustainably, learns from every trade, and
never suffers an unrecoverable loss.

A missed trade costs nothing. A wrong trade costs capital. When in doubt, skip.

## The Two-Account Structure
Engine Account (IBKR): active trading capital. ZEUS has full access.
Vault Account (separate bank): protected profits only. ZEUS never touches this.
At every milestone: 30% of profits move to the Vault. Permanently.

## Position Sizing by Stage
The system automatically enforces these limits. Never override them manually.

SEED (€100 → €1,000):
- Max 1% per trade (€1 at seed)
- Only STRONG conviction signals (tier 1)
- No leverage. No exceptions.
- Kill switch at 5% drawdown — tighter than any other stage

SPRINT (€1,000 → €10,000):
- Max 2% per trade
- STRONG + MODERATE signals (tier 1 + 2)
- No leverage still
- Kill switch at 6% drawdown

SCALE (€10,000 → €100,000):
- Max 3% per trade
- 1.5x leverage on tier-1 only
- Kill switch at 7% drawdown

SERIOUS (€100,000 → €1,000,000):
- Max 5% per trade
- 2x leverage on tier-1 only
- Kill switch at 8% drawdown

EMPIRE (€1,000,000+):
- Max 3% per trade (tighter — protect the empire)
- Kill switch back to 5% — fortress mode

## What "Never Lose Everything" Actually Means
It does not mean zero drawdown is possible — that is mathematically impossible in trading.
It means: losses are bounded, recoverable, and never touch the Vault.
It means: the system always has enough capital to continue trading.
It means: the kill switch activates before a bad run becomes a catastrophe.

A 5% drawdown at seed means €5 lost from €100. The system halts, resets, resumes.
A 5% drawdown at empire means €50,000 from €1,000,000. Same discipline, different scale.

At 2% monthly return, €100 becomes:
- 6 months: €112

- 2 years: €161
- 5 years: €269
- 10 years: €724

At 5% monthly return (aggressive but achievable with strong signals):
- 6 months: €134
- 1 year: €179
- 2 years: €323
- 3 years: €579
- 5 years: €1,868

The lesson: do not rush. The compounding accelerates automatically.
Trying to force 20% monthly will blow the account before year 1.

## When to Skip a Trade
- Confidence below threshold for current stage → SKIP
- VIX above 35 → SUPPRESS ALL
- Bear regime + VIX above 25 → SUPPRESS POSITIVE SIGNALS
- Drawdown approaching kill switch → SKIP until reset
- Signal category has negative historical hit rate → SKIP
- Macro contradicts signal direction → SKIP

Skipping is not failure. Skipping is discipline.
Every skipped bad trade is a preserved euro that compounds into future gains.

## The Feedback Loop
Every trade — win or loss — feeds back into Pythia's memory.
Over time the system learns which signal types, regimes, and VIX bands produce wins.
It automatically allocates more size to proven patterns and less to unproven ones.
This is the learning. It takes time. Give it 3-6 months before judging performance.
